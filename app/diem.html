Start your financial discipline journey with DIEM!

DIEM (Daily Income & Expense Manager) is an app optimised for the time it takes to add a new transaction. When generating data is so simple, making data-driven personal finance decisions is easier than ever.

About DIEM

This app has been carefully designed to make sure that you can add your day to day transactions with the minimum number of clicks, thereby saving you a lot of precious time and allowing you to use this app on-the-fly. In fact, the lesser the time you need to spend on this app, the better we'll consider our product to be. The emphasis has been strongly placed on function over form. The user-interface is simple, straight-forward and elegant. Have a look at the video below to understand how to use this app.

Features

Powerful features that make the app ideal for daily transactions management

Add transactions in the minumum possible clicks

Features like a frequent categories list and the 'Add Transaction' screen being the default screen, make adding a new entry fairly quick

Modify/ Delete Transactions

All components of a transaction (amount, date, category, etc.) are editable

Alarm Notifications

The app will remind you to enter your daily transactions, in case you forget!

Export Data as CSV

Prefer the Excel format? You can export your data as CSV and open in any spreadsheet app!

Statistics and Aggregates in a single swipe

Get your stats and aggregates for various date ranges with a single swipe
